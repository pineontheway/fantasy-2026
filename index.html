Uncapped Bonus

2x Uncapped players get all points doubled if they hit any of these thresholds:
⚡ 30+ runs scored
⚡ 3+ wickets taken
⚡ 3+ fielding dismissals (catches + runouts + stumpings)
If none of these conditions are met, uncapped players earn at 1x (normal rate).
